## Content Strategy for SEO

### Topic Clusters (5 Pillars)

1. **Developer Hiring Costs** (pillar page)
   - Cost of hiring a developer
   - Agency fees vs direct hiring
   - Cost of bad hires in tech

2. **Skills-Based Hiring** (pillar page)
   - Skills vs credentials
   - Work sample assessment
   - Outcome-based evaluation

3. **Junior Developer Career** (pillar page)
   - First developer job
   - Entry-level experience paradox
   - Portfolio vs real experience

4. **Developer Assessment** (pillar page)
   - Technical interview alternatives
   - Work trial best practices
   - Outcome criteria design

5. **Developer Upskilling** (pillar page)
   - Learning by doing vs courses
   - Career switching into tech
   - Real-world project experience

### E-E-A-T Strategy
- **Experience**: Feature real user stories and outcomes
- **Expertise**: Publish data-driven content (leverage research in docs/seo/)
- **Authoritativeness**: Have real developers and hiring managers author content
- **Trust**: Show verified outcome examples, transparent pricing

## Russian Market Considerations

If targeting Russian-speaking developers:
- **Telegram** is #1 channel (30M+ users in Russia)
- **Habr Career** (27.5M monthly visits) for IT job search
- **hh.ru** (48M monthly visits) for general job search
- Key motivation: access to international companies, stable-currency payment
- Consider `/promo/ru` variant in the future
