# Pain Point: The True Cost of Hiring Developers

## Summary

Hiring a software developer costs $4,700-$36,000+ in direct costs before the new hire
writes a single line of code. When bad hires, vacancy costs, and turnover are included,
the total can exceed $260,000.

## Direct Cost Breakdown

### Average Cost-Per-Hire
- **All industries (SHRM)**: $4,700 in 2024 (+17.5% YoY), $4,800 in 2025-2026
- **Tech/engineering roles**: ~$6,200 per hire (vs $2,700 for retail)
- **Executive hires**: $28,329 average (up 113% since 2017)

### Recruiting Agency Fees
- **Contingency agencies**: 15-25% of first-year salary (most common: 20%)
- **Specialized tech roles**: 20-30% of salary
- **Retained search**: 25-35% of salary
- **Concrete example**: $120K developer salary = $18,000-$36,000 in agency fees
- **Silicon Valley premium**: ~40% higher than mid-market cities

### Alternative Recruiting Models
- Flat-fee recruiting: $5,000-$20,000 per hire
- Hourly recruiting: $75-$250/hr, typically $3,000-$20,000 total
- In-house recruiter: $70K-$90K/year salary, handles 20-40 hires/year = $2K-$5K overhead per hire

### Job Boards and Tools
- LinkedIn Recruiter seat: $8,000-$12,000/year
- Job board postings: $200-$500 each
- ATS software: additional recurring cost

> Sources: SHRM Benchmarking Report 2024; Dover Tech Recruiter Fees Guide 2025;
> Alcor IT Recruitment Agency Fees; TalentLeverage 2025

## Time-to-Hire

- **Average time to hire a developer**: 30-41 days (most sources: 35-36 days)
- **Median**: 41 days; slowest 10%: up to 82 days
- **Senior roles**: ~20% longer than junior roles
- **Cost of vacancy**: ~$500/day in lost productivity (Deloitte 2024)
- **41-day process = ~$20,500** in lost productivity per open position

## Hidden Costs

### Candidate Drop-Off (Wasted Investment)
- **60%** of candidates abandon the process due to length/complexity
- **42%** withdraw because scheduling takes too long
- **32%** of withdrawn candidates accepted another offer elsewhere
- **Half of all employers** have lost quality talent due to poor process

### Onboarding and Ramp-Up
- Onboarding adds **20-25% of base salary** ($26K-$32K for a $130K developer)
- In Europe: ~EUR 20,000 per developer onboarding
- Total first-year cost: **$130,000-$180,000+**

## Cost Escalation Trends

- SHRM cost-per-hire rose **17.5% year-over-year** (2023-2024)
- Executive hiring costs up **113% since 2017**
- IT skills shortage projected to cause **$5.5 trillion in global losses** by 2026 (IDC)
- **87%** of tech leaders report challenges finding skilled workers (Robert Half)
- **65%** of IT leaders said hiring was harder in 2025 than 2024
